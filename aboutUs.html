About Us




Our Story

1991: four craftsmen, a rented garage, and the faith that furniture design still leaves a lot to be desired, we begin.

“What if our inspiration is drawn from our surroundings?” Rich in history, with an array of diverse arts and crafts, our designers felt
the potential of these enormous resources.

We could also see the great skill of our local craftspeople, and what we could achieve together with fresh creativity and design
guidance.

Today, fortunately, those styles have become our marks of distinction. We combine them with the best of the classics and
contemporaries to offer an eclectic style of living, often described as: different, distinct and unique.

Now with over several thousands of houses furnished by us, an impressive list of celebrity customers, and eight national awards we
invite you to share ideas at any of our outlets worldwide.

Warmest regards,

Qais Rahman
